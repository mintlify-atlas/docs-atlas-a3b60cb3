# Welcome to Who To Bother

**Who To Bother** is a community-maintained directory that helps developers find the right people to reach out to at tech companies on X (formerly Twitter).

Whether you need to report a bug, share product feedback, ask technical questions, or engage with the community, this platform makes it easy to discover the right contacts at companies like Cloudflare, GitHub, Vercel, and many more.

## Why Who To Bother?

Finding the right person to contact at a tech company can be challenging. Official support channels are often slow, and it's not always clear who handles what. Who To Bother solves this by:

- **Centralizing contact information** — Browse a directory of tech companies with categorized contacts
- **Showing what they work on** — Each contact is associated with specific products or areas
- **Community-driven accuracy** — Anyone can contribute updates to keep information current
- **Open source transparency** — All data is validated and stored in JSON files on GitHub

## How It Works

1. **Browse or Search** — Find the company you're interested in
2. **View Contacts** — See categorized lists of X handles organized by product or role
3. **Reach Out** — Connect with the right person on X for your specific need
4. **Contribute** — Help keep the directory accurate by submitting updates

## Community-Driven

Who To Bother is built and maintained by the developer community. All company data is stored in JSON files and validated using [Valibot](https://valibot.dev) schemas to ensure accuracy and consistency.

<Note>
  This is a community resource and is not officially affiliated with any listed companies. For official support, please visit the respective company websites.
